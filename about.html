Our Mission

To provide comprehensive safety management solutions that protect workers, ensure regulatory compliance, and create a culture of safety excellence.

PGH Risk & Safety is an independent safety consulting firm serving the construction and industrial sectors. With years of hands-on experience, we understand the real-world challenges of maintaining safety on active job sites.

Our approach combines practical field experience with comprehensive regulatory knowledge to deliver safety solutions that work. We don't just check boxes – we build safety cultures that protect your most valuable assets: your people.

Our Services

Site Safety Audits

Comprehensive evaluations to identify hazards and compliance gaps.

Safety Training

OSHA 10/30, fall protection, hazard communication, and more.

Program Development

Custom safety programs tailored to your operations.

Incident Investigation

Root cause analysis and corrective action development.
